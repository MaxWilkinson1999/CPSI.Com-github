Our Story

Carolina piping systems was founded in 1992 in Charlotte North Carolina by two brothers, Mark and Johnny Wilkinson. Together they bring with them over 70 collective years of experience and relationships in the industrial contracting field. Over the years CPSI has become an industry leader in the field of industrial process piping , specializing in industries such as textile, chemical and food processing. We offer a high degree of expertise in the mechanical installation and maintenance of industrial refrigeration systems and the building, mechanical installation and maintenance of ultra low temp Spiral freezers.

Our company works throughout the US and has successfully completed projects in many many states from Maine to California.

We are constantly stirving for true perfection in our craftsmanship, performance, safety and efficiency on every project we undertake.

We have been blessed to have 3 generations of family that have been involved in the day to day operations of CPSI. And many of our team member employees have remained at the company for decades to become CPSI family. They are truly the heart and soul of our operation and provide our customers with consistency and familiarity throughout all of the project services we provide.
